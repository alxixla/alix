    ABOUT ME

    Hello! I'm Alix, a self-taught front-end developer, painter and drawer. My coding journey began in 2019 during the Covid-19 pandemic. Stuck at home, I turned to YouTube videos to grasp the fundamentals of web development. Soon thereafter, I embarked on building my own website to showcase my art and found immense joy in the process. Eager to expand my skills, I enrolled in a free online course on FreeCodeCamp, which greatly enhanced my coding abilities. Subsequently, I acquired a Certificate in Responsive Design, and ever since, I've dedicated myself to coding and mastering new technologies nearly every day.
